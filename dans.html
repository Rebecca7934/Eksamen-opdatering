Dans, dans, dans

We developed a website for a fictive dance school, in order to solve their problem, which was that it was hard to find a tango partner. We developed a concept of an app, that would match you to your perfect dance partner, which we showed through the website that we build. Through the app you would be able to add things such as skill level, your goal, style, location and more. I was in charge of ideation and visual identity, and worked in a team of four peopple.
