LivsLæring

We had to come up with an idea and create a digital solution. We made “LivsLæring” an online learning concept for kids to teach them important life lessons, that they don’t learn in school. These subjects would be such as mental health, discrimination, sexuality, love, and more. We felt it would be useful in this day and age, and make a difference not only to the induvidual person, but also to the people around them as well as their comunity. Along side the actual idea we also created a salesplatform, logo and content plan. I was the project manager in this project and had a team of five people
